Introduction

Hello, my name is Aruna Andrew and I am currently enrolled in a UX/UI boot camp program.

I believe that these qualities are essential in any field, but especially in UX/UI design, where understanding the user's needs and desires is paramount. I am excited to bring my experience and skills to the table and work collaboratively to create designs that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. .

I love the internet, technology, and building beautiful things.
