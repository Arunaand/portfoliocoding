Case Study 2:Mobile Travel App.

MOBILE PROTOTYPE: Mobile Travel App prototype has been designed to help the users have a seamless and effortless travel booking experience.

GOAL: The goal is to create a mobile travel app to help our Low Budget users to find the destination of their choice, book a low budget flight, rent a car, and find a place to stay that is closest to the activities of their choice.


MY ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES Role:
UX designer (Individual Project).
